Yara Du Verre

By: Aleesa Bautista

Former captain to the Anomalous Investigation Institute’s US Branch, Aether Academy, West Division, she was transferred to Darkwick Academy under the belief that she contracted a demon. Soul weighed into Frostheim among the elite, she’s determined to stay hidden in the shadows—finding sanctuary in growing mysterious flora in the offlimits and abandoned Frostheim Greenhouse or turning the former House doctor’s lab into an alchemist’s apothecary.
